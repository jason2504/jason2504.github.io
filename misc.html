Personal Design Interests

Gaming Controller Surface Modeling & Rendering

Developed advanced surface modeling skills through complex gaming controller design, featuring organic ergonomic forms and intricate detail work. This project demonstrates proficiency in SolidWorks advanced surfacing techniques including lofts, sweeps, and boundary surfaces, combined with professional-grade KeyShot rendering capabilities to create photorealistic visualizations with custom materials and lighting setups.

CNC Machined Guitar Multi-Tool

Designed and machined a functional guitar-shaped multi-tool using precision CNC milling techniques. This project showcases advanced CAM programming, toolpath optimization, and material removal strategies while maintaining tight tolerances for functional features. The design integrates multiple utility functions within an aesthetically pleasing form factor, demonstrating both technical machining skills and creative problem-solving.

WALL-E Inspired Mechatronic System

Designed and fabricated a functional WALL-E replica featuring 2-DOF servo-actuated movement with potentiometer feedback control. The system integrates 3D-printed PLA components with micro servo motors, demonstrating mechatronic design principles including torque analysis, sensor integration, and embedded programming. This project showcases the intersection of character design aesthetics with precision mechanical engineering and control systems.

Microrobot Platform Development

Developed a precision 3D-printed platform system for microrobot experiments, featuring z-axis actuation mechanisms and integrated LED illumination control. The modular design accommodates optical equipment while providing controlled environments for robot actuation and observation. This work demonstrates miniaturization challenges, precision manufacturing tolerances, and system integration for distributed robotics research applications.
