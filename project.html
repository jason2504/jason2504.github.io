Performance Analysis of Boids Algorithm in Coverage Scenarios

MEAM 6240: Distributed Robotics (Mar 2025 - Apr 2025)
This project focuses on optimizing the Boids algorithm for efficient area coverage using a swarm of robots. We implemented and tested the algorithm in various scenarios, including a cafeteria setting, to evaluate its effectiveness in real-world applications. The results demonstrate the potential of Boids for tasks such as environmental monitoring and search-and-rescue operations.
